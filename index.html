USC Singapore Student Association

A place for Singaporeans, Singapore PRs and whoever interested in anything Singaporean!

SSA organizes social, networking and just plain fun events, geared towards promoting Singaporean culture, building a strong Singaporean community in L.A., and providing networking opportunities for students with local and overseas alumni.

SSA organizes social, networking and just plain fun events, geared towards promoting Singaporean culture, building a strong Singaporean community in L.A., and providing networking opportunities for students with local and overseas alumni.

Our main events include an annual Chinese New Year banquet, semesterly Welcome Beach Bonfire, and a yearly Thanksgiving Dinner, alongside many other activities throughout the school year.

So come down to our next event and check us out!
